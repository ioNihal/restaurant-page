ABOUT US

Sathaar Bucks is a family-owned restaurant that serves delicious and wholesome food with a touch of Indian flair. Whether you are looking for a hearty breakfast, a refreshing dessert, or a satisfying dinner, we have something for everyone. Our menu features a variety of dishes, from the classic Shepherd’s Pie and Risotto alla Milanese, to the exotic Royal Butter Chicken and Tuna Nicoise Salad Bowl. We also offer a range of beverages, including coffee, tea, juice, and smoothies. At Sathaar Bucks, we use fresh and quality ingredients, and we prepare our food with love and care. Come and join us for a memorable dining experience, where families find food and happiness.
